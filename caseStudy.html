PsychGigs

A hiring platform for mental health professionals focused on clarity, trust, and ease of use.

  • Role UI/UX Designer
  • Platform Web Application
  • Timeline 2–3 Weeks
  • Scope UX · UI · Design System

Overview

PsychGigs helps clinics and organizations hire qualified mental health professionals faster. The goal was to design a clean, professional interface that simplifies the hiring journey while building trust.

Challenges & Objectives

  • Limited time for research and discovery
  • Initial design direction misaligned with business goals
  • No existing visuals or design assets

The objective was to create a clear and structured experience that reduces friction, improves understanding, and supports faster decision-making.

Research & Strategy

We aligned quickly through kickoff discussions and UI-first exploration. Comparable healthcare and hiring platforms were reviewed to define layout, hierarchy, and interaction patterns.

Design Contribution

Simplified user flows to reduce cognitive load.

Built reusable UI components for consistency.

Delivered clean layouts for smooth developer handoff.

Outcome
